Frequently Asked Questions(FAQ's)

What is a Metamask wallet?

Brief: Metamask is your digital key to the blockchain world.

Function: Manages cryptocurrency and interacts with Ethereum DApps.

How do I create a Metamask wallet?

Process: Visit metamask.io, install the extension, and set up with a secure password.

Essential: Save the secret backup phrase for wallet access.

Where can I get testnet tokens?

Source: Use faucets like faucets.chain.link or faucet.ropsten.be.

Free: Obtain testnet tokens by entering your wallet address.

What's the purpose of testnet tokens?

Developer Playground: Test and experiment with your DApp risk-free.

Safe Testing: Create a secure environment for DApp trial and error.

Can I vote for multiple leaders?

Voters are allowed to cast votes for multiple leaders in each election, promoting a fair, transparent, and individualized voting process while preventing manipulation.

Can I customize moderation settings?

Moderation Control:Utilize a dedicated dashboard for monitoring.

Adaptability: Implement customizable moderation rules for different scenarios.

Prompt Action:Enable real-time notifications for quick issue resolution.

How do I handle disputes in voting results?

Transparent Process:Establish a clear and transparent dispute resolution process.

Community Involvement:Involve the community in dispute resolution for fairness.

Smart Contract Support:Leverage smart contracts for automated dispute resolution.

How can I confirm that my vote has been successfully recorded?

Confirmation Receipt:Upon submitting a vote, users receive a confirmation receipt with a unique transaction ID.

Blockchain Transparency:Users can verify their vote on the blockchain, leveraging the transparency of the technology.

Accessible Records:Accessible records provide assurance that each vote is accurately recorded and counted.

Can I transfer testnet tokens to someone else?

UTestnet tokens are generally meant for individual testing.

If you want to simulate transactions between users, create multiple Metamask accounts to interact within your DApp.
